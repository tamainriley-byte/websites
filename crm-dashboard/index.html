Championship Track

Dials become bookings, bookings become seen appointments, appointments become opportunities — and the library and exam hall pay XP too. Funnel bars are scaled against the squad's best at each stage.

Where the pipeline comes from matters: every opportunity is tagged to its source — client reviews, new leads, estate-planner handovers or revived lost opportunities. Fast task resolution earns a standing speed bonus.

Membership Care wins on speed and on keeping the review book full: the faster tickets close, the bigger the standing bonus — and every review scheduled feeds the advisers' pipeline.
