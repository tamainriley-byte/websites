# Google Ads — keyword-matched ad plan

Account **806-912-8725**, campaign **"Calm & Contour - Search"**. This plan replaces the
single catch-all ad group with **seven themed ad groups**, each mapped 1:1 to a service
page, so the searcher's keyword appears in the ad title and body, and the click lands on
a page carrying the same message with a clear CTA (every green button opens the AI chat,
which books directly into Parissa's calendar).

Strategy (from what we learned): specific "money" phrases are Low Search Volume as exact
match, so run keywords as **broad match inside tightly themed ad groups**, protected by
the shared **negative keyword list** at the bottom. Pause the old generic keywords
(`massage studio`, `massage and wellness`) — they bring wrong-fit studio traffic.

All headlines ≤30 characters, descriptions ≤90 characters (validated). Pin nothing;
let Google rotate. Use sitelinks to the other service pages.
